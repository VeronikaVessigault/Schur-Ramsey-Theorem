Bulk Random Walk

All statistics of the runs you choose to do will be provided to you once they are completed.
Those statistics will then be added to the total statistics page.

Please carefully review the allowed inputs below.
1. Random Walks for dimensions 1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6, 7, and 8 are supported.
2. The number of steps should be an integer either (between 200 and 7500 inclusive AND divisible by 100) OR (between 7500 and 15000 inclusive AND divisible by 500).
3. The number of walks should be an integer between 1 and 25000 inclusive.







Please click the button only once, and wait for the statistics to show.
For large number of runs and steps this can take 1-5 minutes.
